<objective>
Replace `useBreathingSessionController.ts`'s boundary-detection effect (L325-364) with a top-up trigger that calls `engine.topUpLookahead(cues)` with a pre-computed cue list from a new pure domain helper `walkFutureCues` in `domain/sessionAudio.ts`. Purpose: With Plan 01 done, the engine can dispatch N cues ahead via `topUpLookahead`. This plan wires the CALLER side: on every controller tick (driven by `session.liveFrame` or `session.currentFrame`), compute the next N cue audioTimes from the anchor and dispatch. The "boundary-detected one cue at a time" model from v2.1 is replaced by a "queue depth target" model. Closes SCHED-02 (lookahead window queues N cues ahead) + SCHED-03 (audio plays through hidden window — short windows) + SCHED-04 (clean stop after the lookahead exhausts — automatic per D-12 envelope self-termination).

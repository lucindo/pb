THEME-05 contrast guard (src/styles/theme.contrast.test.ts):
- The test runs `describe.each(CONCRETE_THEMES)` over 5 themes (light/dark/moss/slate/dusk).
- For each: parses `--color-orb-in-from` + `--color-orb-in-to` -> midpoint; same for out;
  asserts `contrastRatio(inMid, outMid) >= 1.5`.

  plan does NOT touch accent/accent-strong/on-accent tokens.

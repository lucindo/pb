<objective>
Create the pure-data timbre preset module `src/audio/timbres.ts` — the single source of truth for the four named timbre DSP recipes (Bowl, Bell, Sine, Chime). The Bowl preset is the verbatim move of the existing module-level constants from `cueSynth.ts` (TIMBRE-02 byte-identical proof at the data layer; the consumer-side proof lands in Plan 03). The three new presets (Bell, Sine, Chime) honor TIMBRE-01 (four named presets) and TIMBRE-05 (A4 In / A3 Out fundamental per preset). A guard test in `timbres.test.ts` (D-21) prevents future preset additions from silently breaking the fundamental invariant.

Purpose: Land the foundational data module that downstream plans (03 cueSynth dispatch, 04 useAudioCues capture, 05 TimbrePicker, 06 App integration) consume without re-deriving DSP values. Zero React, zero Web Audio side effects — pure constants only.

Output: NEW `src/audio/timbres.ts` (~50 LOC) + NEW `src/audio/timbres.test.ts` (~40 LOC). No edits to `cueSynth.ts` (deferred to Plan 03 — moving the constants there happens when the consumer is wired up, so this plan can commit independently green).
</objective>

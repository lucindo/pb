Add the session-scoped timbre capture site to `useAudioCues.ts` — the canonical capture-at-Start mechanism for TIMBRE-03. Mirror of Phase 5.1's `mutedRef` synchronous-pre-await posture: a new `timbreRef = useRef<TimbreId>(DEFAULT_TIMBRE)` is set BEFORE the first `await` in `start(plan, timbre)`, and read BEFORE the first `await` in `reconstructEngine()`.

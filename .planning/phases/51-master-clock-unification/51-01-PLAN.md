<objective>
Land the `createSwappableSessionClock` proxy primitive used by Phase 51 audio hooks (D-03/D-04). The proxy is a stable `SessionClock` whose internal source can be swapped via `setSource(next)` â€” the externally-visible `clock` reference NEVER changes identity, so `useSessionEngine.ts`'s `clock` dep at L186 and `useNKEngine.ts`'s `clock` dep at L174 do NOT thrash when the audio hooks swap from a wall-clock initial source to the audio-backed clock at AC construction (D-05 / D-06) or to a freshly-reconstructed engine's clock at iOS recovery (D-11).

Subscriptions registered via `onSuspend` / `onResume` / `onClose` MUST survive a source swap â€” the proxy maintains its own subscriber Sets and re-subscribes them on the new source whenever `setSource` is called. Without this, the iOS recovery subscriptions in `useAudioCues.ts:296-299` would silently drop on AC reconstruction.

Purpose: Single-source-of-truth primitive for the Phase 51 dual-source-lifecycle bridging pattern. Plans 02 + 03 consume this in `useAudioCues` and `useNaviKriyaAudio`.

# v2.2 Audio Sync — Milestone Audit

## Verdict: PASSED

The milestone goal — **the breathing app's audio works correctly on desktop and mobile** — is achieved and operator-verified on device. No critical gaps. No outstanding tech debt (the lint drift found during audit was cleared at close).

## What shipped (operator-verified)
- **No per-breath tick** — one clean strike per in/out boundary (in-flight boundary-cue dedup).
- **Background audio continuity (desktop)** — a Web Worker heartbeat keeps the cue queue topped up while a tab is hidden; audio plays for the full session instead of dying after the ~6s lookahead.
- **Resume in sync** — the resume clamp was removed; the orb tracks true audio time and snaps to the live position on return. Mobile (iOS) preserves practice-time (AC suspends → clock freezes) with no special-casing.
- **Instant mute/unmute** — a single master GainNode; mute/unmute is a 0.05s ramp; unmute lands on the currently-playing cue with no boundary wait.
